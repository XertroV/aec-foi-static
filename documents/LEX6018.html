FOI Request LEX6018

Date: 2024

The FOI request sought statistical data concerning responses to penalty notices issued to non-voters in the 2023 referendum for the electoral division of Lingiari. The request aimed to understand how non-voters addressed these notices, specifically categorizing them by those who paid an administrative penalty, provided a valid reason for not voting, or did not respond.

The following documents were released in part under Section 22:
* "Search results: response to penalty notices regarding the 2023 referendum for the division of Lingiari."
* "Search results: non-voters regarding the 2023 referendum who provided a valid reason not to vote for the division of Lingiari."

The main content of the released documents indicates the following statistics for the 2023 referendum in the division of Lingiari:
* Out of a total of 9110 non-voters who received penalty notices:
* 588 individuals provided a valid reason for not voting (VS).
* 865 individuals responded by paying the $20 administrative penalty (PP).
* 5592 individuals did not respond to the first penalty notice (NR).
* A separate statistical report specifically confirms 588 valid replies were entered, aligning with the number of individuals who provided a valid reason for not voting.
