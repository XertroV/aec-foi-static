FOI Request LEX452

Date: 2021

FOI Request Summary: Zi Chun Wang Donations 2013/2014

This summary outlines the main purpose, released documents, and key content related to FOI Request LEX452 concerning donations made by Zi Chun Wang.

Main Purpose of the FOI Request

The Freedom of Information (FOI) request, identified as LEX452 (Record 55-SVNG9), sought documents related to donations made by Zi Chun Wang for the financial year ending 2013/2014.

Documents from the FOI Request

The released documents consist of a schedule detailing three specific communications from the Australian Electoral Commission (AEC) to Zi Wang:

  • 22/10/2014: Letter from AEC to Zi Wang regarding his obligation to complete a Donor to Political Party Disclosure Return – Individuals for the 2013/2014 financial year.
  • 14/11/2014: Letter from AEC to Zi Wang serving as a reminder to lodge the Donor to Political Party Disclosure Return – Individuals for the 2013/2014 financial year.
  • 17/11/2014: Acknowledgement letter from AEC to Mr Wang, confirming receipt of his Individual Donor Return for the 2013-2014 financial year.

The release also includes pages detailing redaction codes, indicating that certain types of information (e.g., personal information, business information) may be withheld from the full documents.

Main Content from the FOI Request Documents

The core content revealed by these documents pertains to the Australian Electoral Commission's process of ensuring compliance with donor disclosure requirements for Zi Chun Wang for the 2013/2014 financial year:

  • The AEC initiated contact with Zi Wang to inform him of his legal obligation to disclose donations.
  • Following the initial notification, a reminder was sent, indicating the ongoing need for the return to be lodged.
  • Ultimately, the AEC confirmed that Zi Wang submitted the required Individual Donor Return for the 2013-2014 financial year, thereby fulfilling his disclosure obligation.
