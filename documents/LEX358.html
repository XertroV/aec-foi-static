FOI Request LEX358

Date: 2021

  • Main Purpose of the FOI Request:
    The FOI request, identified as LEX359, sought the metadata (specifically timestamps) related to an email from the Liberal Party ("Old Libs"). This email, an "Objection - The New Liberals," was previously published as a redacted document on the Australian Electoral Commission (AEC) website. The requester aimed to obtain detailed timestamp information surrounding the email.

  • Documents from the FOI Request:
    One document was released under the FOI request:

    • Document No. 1: "Metadata retrieved in respect of the email dated 19 March 2021 8:45 AM from Andrew Hirst, Federal Director, Liberal Party of Australia" (Undated release).

  • Main Content from the FOI Request Documents:
    The released document contains the full email headers for the "Objection - The New Liberals.msg" email. Key details extracted from these headers include:

    • Sender: Andrew Hirst (Andrew.Hirst@liberal.org.au), Federal Director, Liberal Party of Australia.
    • Recipient: fad@aec.gov.au.
    • Subject: Objection - "The New Liberals".
    • Key Timestamps (all in Australian Eastern Daylight Time, AEDT, +1100 unless specified):
      • Sender's Internal System (LPAEX01.liberal.local): The email originated and was sent from the Liberal Party's internal server at Fri, 19 Mar 2021 08:45:21 +1100. The Date: header, which is typically the time the email was composed/sent by the client, shows Thu, 18 Mar 2021 21:45:21 +0000 (UTC), which converts to 08:45:21 AEDT on March 19.
      • Liberal Party's External Gateway (external.liberal.org.au): Fri, 19 Mar 2021 08:45:23 +1100 (AEDT).
      • MailGuard (mg-auso-delta.mailguard.com.au): Received from the Liberal Party's gateway at Fri, 19 Mar 2021 08:45:23 +1100 (AEDT).
      • AEC's Mail Gateway (mailin-aec2.humanservices.gov.au): Received from MailGuard at Fri, 19 Mar 2021 08:45:34 +1100 (AEDT).
      • AEC's Internal Mail Server (aecmail.prod.aec.gov.au): The final receipt timestamp by the AEC's core mail server was Fri, 19 Mar 2021 08:48:38 +1100.
    • Originating IP (internal to sender's network): [192.168.8.77].
    • Attachments: The header X-MS-Has-Attach: yes indicates the email contained attachments.
