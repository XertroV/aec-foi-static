FOI Request LEX6188

Date: 2024

The FOI request primarily sought information regarding non-voters in the 2023 referendum for the division of Canberra.

The documents released in response to the request are:
* "Search results: response to penalty notices regarding the 2023 referendum for the division of Canberra."
* "Statistics on non-voters regarding the 2023 referendum who provided a valid reason not to vote."

The main content from the released documents pertains to the categorized responses of individuals who did not vote in the 2023 referendum in the division of Canberra. This includes data on:
* Those who paid the $20 administrative penalty (PP).
* Those who provided a valid reason for not voting (VS).
* Those who did not respond to the first penalty notice (NR).
Additionally, broader statistics are provided on non-voters who offered a valid reason for their non-participation. Both documents were released in part under Section 22.
