FOI Request LEX6205

Date: 2024

FOI Request Summary

  • Main Purpose of FOI Request: The request, identified as FOI-2024-00055, sought to determine the total number of individuals removed from the UK between January 1, 2024, and April 1, 2024, with a breakdown by country of origin.

  • Documents from the FOI Request: The provided document is a response letter dated May 6, 2024, from the Home Office Freedom of Information Team to the applicant of FOI-2024-00055.

  • Main Content:

    • The Home Office acknowledged receipt of the request on April 16, 2024, and confirmed that it holds the requested information.
    • The request was refused under Section 21 (Information accessible by other means) of the Freedom of Information Act 2000.
    • The Home Office stated that immigration statistics, including data on enforced removals and voluntary departures, are published quarterly and annually on GOV.UK under the 'Immigration statistics' section.
    • The specific data for the requested period (January to April 2024) was not yet available at the time of the response.
    • It was advised that this data would be published in August 2024 as part of the quarterly release 'Immigration statistics, year ending June 2024'.
    • A direct link to the relevant GOV.UK statistics collection was provided: https://www.gov.uk/government/collections/immigration-statistics.
    • The letter also informed the applicant of their right to request an internal review within 28 calendar days.
