FOI Request LEX890

Date: 2021

FOI Request Summary

  • Main Purpose of the FOI Request: The FOI request, submitted by Peter Chiam (LEX890), sought the document referred to as the ‘Exchange of Information Agreement with the New South Wales Electoral Commission’.

  • Documents from the FOI Request: One document was identified and retrieved:

    • Description: AEC-NSWEC Exchange of Information Agreement 2020-23
    • Date: 03/02/2020

  • Main Content from the FOI Request Documents: The provided documents confirm the successful identification and retrieval of the requested ‘AEC-NSWEC Exchange of Information Agreement 2020-23’. However, the actual content of the agreement itself is not included in the provided pages (pages 2-12 appear blank or only contain page numbers), only its description and date. The documents primarily serve as a record of the FOI request and the scheduled document identified for release.
